RSS Reader

RSS Reader for local Markdown notes

Read RSS feeds in a full-screen Mark It Down workspace, import OPML, inspect articles, and save selected items to local Markdown notes in Inbox.

~ ~ ~
Workspace

A reader surface inside the editor, not another inbox to ignore.

RSS Reader gives feeds their own full-screen workspace: feed list on the left, article list and reading pane in the center, and metadata on the right.

  • Full-screen reading — Move from feed list to article view without opening another app or browser tab.
  • Article inspector — Review metadata, tags, and headings before deciding whether the article deserves a note.
  • Inbox save — Save only the article you choose, then rewrite it as a normal local Markdown note.
Subscriptions

Subscriptions stay portable. Article bodies stay intentional.

RSS settings can move with you, while article content is saved only when you explicitly clip it. That keeps the reader useful without turning it into a hidden bulk archive.

  • OPML import and export — Bring subscriptions from Feedly, Inoreader, NetNewsWire, and other OPML 2.0 readers.
  • Git sync for settings — Sync feeds and denylist settings across devices while keeping permissions device-local.
  • Web subscriptions — For sites without RSS, fetch article URLs and titles first; save body text only when you explicitly clip.

Turn reading into notes, not a backlog.
